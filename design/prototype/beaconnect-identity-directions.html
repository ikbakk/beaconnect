Beaconnect · identity directions

Calm identity routes for a reassurance-first relationship product.

A Beaconnect mark should feel present, not performative: soft enough to live on onboarding and the launcher icon, restrained enough to disappear once the partner becomes the focus.

No surveillance cues: no pins, radar, targets, pulses, or precision rings.
Mutuality over monitoring: forms should imply two-sided connection, not one-sided tracking.
Warm restraint: muted terracotta, sage, bark, and linen instead of bright trust-tech blues.
Usage discipline: brand shows on onboarding, splash, launcher, About, and store surfaces only.

Recommended default: Route 1, because it matches the current app palette and reads most naturally inside the Android concept you already have.
