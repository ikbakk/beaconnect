# Brand Identity

## Core Statement

> Someone's favorite app to never think about.

Beaconnect should not compete for attention.

It should quietly support relationships and then step aside.

## Brand Voice

Beaconnect speaks like someone who texts:

> No rush, just letting you know I am home.

Voice characteristics:

- calm
- brief
- human
- non-technical
- never dramatic
- never manipulative
- never overly cute

## Visual Personality

Beaconnect should feel:

- quiet
- dependable
- thoughtful
- respectful
- human
- predictable

It should not feel:

- corporate
- gamified
- loud
- flashy
- clinical
- surveillance-oriented

## Brand Rule

> The app is not the main character. The relationship is.

## Logo Usage

Logo appears only in:

- launcher icon
- splash screen
- onboarding
- About screen
- store listing

After onboarding, the partner becomes the identity.

Home starts with the partner, not the app.
