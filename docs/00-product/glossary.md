# Glossary

## Beaconnect Terms

### Check-in

A one-tap expression of presence.

Not a chat message. Not a location dump.

### Live Sharing

Temporary, intentional, higher-frequency sharing for a limited time.

### Current Place

Human-readable place context.

Avoid saying GPS or coordinates to users.

### No New Updates Yet

A calm state indicating freshness has aged.

Avoid saying offline or failed unless necessary.

### Trust Center

A section that explains how Beaconnect works between two people.

### My Beacon

Personal preferences that affect only the user's own experience.

### Pair Symbol

A small user-selected glyph representing the pair.

### Shared Connection

The one-to-one relationship in v1.

### Updates

Human-readable meaningful events.

Not a raw system log.
