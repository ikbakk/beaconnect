# Trust Center v1.0

Status: Frozen

## Purpose

Trust Center answers:

> Can I trust Beaconnect with our relationship?

## Structure

```text
Our Connection
Sharing
Privacy
History
Permissions
How Beaconnect Works
```

## Universal Pattern

```text
Explain
↓
Status
↓
Action
```

## Our Connection

Shows:

- pair name
- connection status
- connected since
- mutual sharing note

## Sharing

Explains:

- sharing is mutual
- live sharing is temporary
- check-ins are intentional

## Privacy

Explains:

- what is shared
- who sees it
- how long it stays
- how to pause/stop

## History

Shows:

- detailed history: 3 days
- monthly summaries
- mutual deletion approval

## Permissions

Explains in human language.

Avoid Android permission constants.

## How Beaconnect Works

Short handbook entries:

- Why Live Sharing is temporary
- Why not every movement is shown
- Why updates may be delayed
- Why consent matters
- How privacy is protected

## Tone

Leave users more informed, never more worried.
